# CTS Customer Operations Portal — Interactive Prototype

## Project Overview

Interactive HTML5 prototype/mockup of the **CTS Customer Operations Portal, Service Desk & Mobile App**. Built for customer pitch presentation to demonstrate the platform's UI, workflows, and capabilities before full-stack development begins.

This is a **front-end prototype only** — no backend, no database, no authentication. All data is simulated with JavaScript. The purpose is to show Jacqueline Bokosi and the CTS team exactly what they will get.

## What This Prototype Demonstrates

1. **Customer Portal** — Parcel tracking, booking, account dashboard
2. **Service Desk** — Ticket creation, SLA status, escalation views
3. **Admin Console** — Branch management, tariff rules, route config
4. **Invoice & Finance** — Invoice viewer, payment status, statements
5. **Mobile App Preview** — Phone-frame mockup of the customer app
6. **Analytics Dashboard** — KPI cards, charts, delivery performance
